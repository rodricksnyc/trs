    The TRS Knowledge Center is currently available for NIH Tobacco Centers of Regulatory Science (TCORS) researchers and trainees.

  • How do I register for an event?

    Go to the Announcements, then Meetings and Events page. Search the list for the event of interest. Click on the event and there will be information about how to register for the event.

  • How do I access a training course or presentation?

    To access a course, go to the Learning Library. You can either search for the course using the Search/Filter function, or you can browse the listing of materials. Click on the course title launch the course or presentation.
